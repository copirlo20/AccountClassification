Khi to m hnh GCN

Graph Convolutional Network (GCN) c xy dng bng th vin PyTorch Geometric vi kin trc gm hai lp tch chp (GCNConv). D liu u vo i qua lp tch chp u tin, sau p dng hm kch hot ReLU v dropout, trc khi chuyn tip n lp tch chp th hai a ra xc sut phn loi. Qu trnh lan truyn thng tin trn th c m t bng cng thc chun ha nh sau:

$$ H^{(l+1)} = \sigma\left( \tilde{D}^{-1/2} \tilde{A} \tilde{D}^{-1/2} H^{(l)} W^{(l)} \right) $$

Trong :

  • $H^{(l)}$: ma trn c trng (feature matrix) ti lp th $l$
  • $W^{(l)}$: ma trn trng s c hc ti lp $l$
  • $\sigma$: hm kch hot phi tuyn, thng l ReLU
  • $\tilde{A} = A + I$: ma trn k (adjacency matrix) thm self-loop
  • $\tilde{D}$: ma trn bc (degree matrix) tng ng vi $\tilde{A}$

M hnh c khi to vi s c trng u vo bng graph.num_node_features, s chiu n l 64, v u ra l s lp phn loi t nhn ca ngi dng. Sau khi khi to, m hnh GCN sn sng hun luyn nhm phn loi ti khon tht v gi trong th ngi dng.

# nh ngha m hnh GCN
class GCN(torch.nn.Module):
    # Phng thc khi to kin trc m hnh GCN
    def __init__(self, in_channels, hidden_channels, num_classes):
        super().__init__()
        self.conv1 = GCNConv(in_channels, hidden_channels)
        self.conv2 = GCNConv(hidden_channels, num_classes)
        
    # Phng thc forward  thc hin qu trnh lan truyn qua cc lp GCNConv
    def forward(self, x, edge_index):
        x = self.conv1(x, edge_index)
        x = F.relu(x)
        x = F.dropout(x, p=0.5, training=self.training)
        x = self.conv2(x, edge_index)
        return x

Hun luyn v nh gi m hnh

Qu trnh hun luyn m hnh bt u bng vic khi to b ti u ha Adam vi tc hc l 0.01 v h s regularization 5e-4 nhm hn ch overfitting. Hm train() thc hin forward pass trn tp train, tnh ton hm mt mt cross-entropy v cp nht trng s thng qua lan truyn ngc. Hm mt mt c nh ngha nh sau:

$$ \mathcal{L}_{\text{train}} = - \sum_{i \in \text{Train}} y_i \cdot \log(\hat{y}_i) $$

Trong khi , hm test() chuyn m hnh sang ch nh gi (eval()), d on nhn bng argmax, ri tnh chnh xc trn c tp hun luyn v kim tra. Cng thc chnh xc l:

$$ \text{Accuracy} = \frac{\text{S lng d on ng}}{\text{Tng s mu trong tp}} $$

Sau nhiu vng lp (epoch), m hnh dn hc c cch phn bit ti khon tht v gi da trn cu trc v c trng ca th ngi dng.

# Khi to b ti u ha Adam vi m hnh v cc tham s hc
optimizer = torch.optim.Adam(model.parameters(), lr=0.01, weight_decay=5e-4)
display(optimizer)

# Phng thc hun luyn m hnh
def train():
    model.train()
    optimizer.zero_grad()
    out = model(graph.x, graph.edge_index)
    loss = F.cross_entropy(out[graph.train_mask], graph.y[graph.train_mask])
    loss.backward()
    optimizer.step()
    return loss.item()

# Phng thc kim tra m hnh
def test():
    model.eval()
    with torch.no_grad():
        out = model(graph.x, graph.edge_index)
        pred = out.argmax(dim=1)
    accs = []
    for mask in [graph.train_mask, graph.val_mask, graph.test_mask]:
        correct = pred[mask] == graph.y[mask]
        acc = int(correct.sum()) / int(mask.sum())
        accs.append(acc)
    return accs

for epoch in range(1, 101):
    loss = train()
    train_acc, val_acc, test_acc = test()
    print(f'Epoch: {epoch:03d}, Loss: {loss:.4f}, Train Acc: {train_acc:.4f},  Val Acc: {val_acc:.4f}, Test Acc: {test_acc:.4f}')
    history.loc[len(history)] = {
        'epoch': epoch,
        'loss': loss,
        'train_acc': train_acc,
        'val_acc': val_acc,
        'test_acc': test_acc
    }
    if val_acc > best_val_acc:
        best_val_acc = val_acc
        counter = 0
        best_model_state = model.state_dict()
    else:
        counter += 1
    if counter >= patience:
        print(f"GCN Early stopped at epoch {epoch} with Val Acc = {best_val_acc:.4f}")
        break
    if epoch == 100:
        print(f"GCN stopped at epoch {epoch} with Val Acc = {best_val_acc:.4f}")

nh gi hiu sut m hnh

Sau khi hun luyn, m hnh c nh gi ton din thng qua nhiu ch s khc nhau. u tin l chnh xc tng th (Accuracy), c tnh bng t l phn trm s mu c phn loi ng:

$$ \text{Accuracy} = \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^N \mathbf{1}(y_i = \hat{y}_i) $$

Ngoi ra, ma trn nhm ln c s dng phn tch chi tit s lng d on ng/sai gia cc lp Real v Fake. Bo co phn loi (Classification Report) cung cp cc ch s quan trng gm Precision, Recall v F1-score:

$$ \text{Precision} = \frac{TP}{TP + FP}, \quad \text{Recall} = \frac{TP}{TP + FN}, \quad \text{F1-score} = 2 \cdot \frac{\text{Precision} \cdot \text{Recall}}{\text{Precision} + \text{Recall}} $$

Cui cng, biu ROC v ch s AUC phn nh kh nng phn bit hai lp ca m hnh, trong AUC c nh ngha l:

$$ \text{AUC} = \int_0^1 \text{TPR}(FPR) \, d(\text{FPR}) $$

Tp hp cc ch s nh lng ny gip nh gi ton din hiu sut ca m hnh trong vic phn loi ti khon tht v gi trn th ngi dng.
